All About Factory Reset, What Is It and What It Does to Your Vivo Y28 5G?

I bought a new phone and want to sell my old Vivo Y28 5G 3, so I need to clear my data off it. Can someone guide me on how to factory reset the Vivo Y28 5G 3 so I can sell it?

Part 1: What is Vivo Y28 5G Factory Reset?

Principles of Vivo Y28 5G Factory Reset

Before you reset your Vivo Y28 5G phone, you must ensure your data is all backed up someplace. It can be either on another device or transferred to the cloud. While changing your phone, you can exchange data from your old Vivo Y28 5G without much stretch. If you own a Vivo Y28 5G, a large portion of your data is put away securely in servers.

One way or another, you must twofold check these things before a factory reset:

  • Google ID
  • Contacts
  • Pictures Gallery
  • Passwords
  • Music

Steps to Start Vivo Y28 5G Factory Reset

Now is the right time to factory reset Vivo Y28 5G when you have everything backed up. The following steps will help you factory reset your Vivo Y28 5G:

  • On your Vivo Y28 5G, head into the settings application. Look over right down and search for System and click it. Look down and search for Reset options.

  • Go to System and tap the “Erase all data” option.

  • Note: After tapping this, the following page will allow you to see what will be cleaned from the Vivo Y28 5G device. Make sure everything is covered in your backup.
  • Pick whether you need to delete any downloaded SIMs and tap Erase all data.

  • You might need to enter your password to complete the reset.

Differences Between Hard Reset and Factory Reset

A factory reset deletes everything from your Vivo Y28 5G device. You might utilize a factory reset when your phone could have a virus or when it is seriously breaking down. A factory reset returns your Vivo Y28 5G to its factory settings, driving you to set it up again as a new phone.

A hard reset is a basic rebooting of your cell phone. It doesn’t influence the data on your Vivo Y28 5G. You might utilize a hard reset when your Vivo Y28 5G is acting slow. However, it is different from a standard reboot of your Vivo Y28 5G.

Part 2: What can Vivo Y28 5G Factory Reset do?

A factory reset deletes every user information, application, related application data, and settings on your pixel phone. It is like hard drive reformatting in a PC. However, a factory reset fixes different hardware and software-related issues. It also unlocks your phone in case you forget your lock screen password, or you need to factory reset Vivo Y28 5G before selling it to protect your privacy.
